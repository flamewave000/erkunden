# Anomalies and Galaxy Events

High security zones are protected from Worm Holes and Anomalies via miniaturized Pulsars. However, Low Security, Contested, and Open Space zones do not contain such protection. This allows for Worm Hole travel, as well as for Anomalies to appear.

Anomalies are several kinds of peculiar events that occur randomly throughout the galaxy.



> Some possible options:
> 
> - Wild Worm Hole: spewing celestial creatures, a sort of "World Boss" can appear. Players can enter the event as a crew and travel through to beat the boss and close the worm hole.
> 
> - Resource Rich Meteor: A massive Asteroid enters a system, pulled by the gravitational field of the system's star. Limited time event to mine as many resources as you can before it meteors into the sun.
> 
> - Marauder Invasion: A marauder faction attempts to take over an occupied system. Players can engage with the event and help the local authorities stop the invasion.
> 
> - Cosmic Beast: A astronomically large creature appears, wondering through a system and gobbling up its resources. High level players can engage the creature, getting massive rewards upon defeating it.

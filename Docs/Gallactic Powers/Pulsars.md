# Pulsars

These high energy Neutron Stars release massive amounts of magnetic radiation. This radiation can disrupt sensors and navigation equipment depending on how energetic the pulsar is.



## Large Pulsars

Large pulsars exist at the center of an Astral System and prevent any form of navigation, sensor readings, and Worm Hole Generation from inside the system. A player must travel to the outer bounds of the system using Sublight Engines to perform travel. When in this area, System Maps are unusable and only short range sensors remain in operation. Naturally, this causes very dangerous environments as anything can be hiding in the dark of the Pulsar system.

## Medium Pulsars

Worm Hole travel and FTL Drives cannot operate inside of these systems and long range communication is not possible. Long Range sensors remain operational however. Players must still travel to the edge of the system with Sublight Engines to perform FTL Travel.

## Small Pulsars

These pulsars prevent Worm Holes from being opened but do not prevent any other ship sensors, systems, or communication. These are often captured and held inside of Astral Systems containing central governments to stop Worm Holes from being opened inside their home system and prevent invasion from enemies.

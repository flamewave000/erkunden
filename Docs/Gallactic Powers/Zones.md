# Zones

Each [Astral System](../Environment/Astral System.md) may contain one or more zones. These zones are occupied by Galactic Governments that maintain power in various systems. Depending on a Player's alliances, these zones can be hostile, friendly, or neutral.

## Friendly Zones

Friendly zones are those that are controlled by an authority the player has a Friendly reputation with. Services found in these zones are available at regular prices and all services are available.

## Hostile Zones

These zones are occupied by an authority the player has a hostile reputation with. These are areas are very dangerous as NPC ships will actively seek out and eliminate players that venture into their domain.

## Contested Zones

Contested zones contain more than one authority that are vying for control of the Astral System. Active battles can be found throughout the system and several warships actively patrol the system.

## Open Space

This is the neutral regions of the galaxy. There are no governing authorities and services can be scarce and at a premium. Free from active patrols, open space is full of wandering [Marauders](../Environment/Marauders.md) and aggressive [Celestial Creatures](../Environment/Celestial Creatures.md). But much can be obtained from the riches found in Open Space.
